I am a recent computer science master's graduate from Brown University with a research interest in Embodied AI. Specifically, I am passionate about expanding the quality and diversity of usable data for robot learning by way of improved data collection methods, data generation methods, representation learning methods, and more.

Prior to my master's degree, I double majored in Computer Science and Mathematics at Carleton College in Northfield, Minnesota. Outside of academics, I enjoy recreational hiking, discussing philosophy and writing short stories.

本人系布朗大学计算机科学专业新晋硕士毕业生，研究方向聚焦于具身智能领域，希望通过数据采集、数据生成、以及表征学习等方面 的创新提升机器人学习可用数据的质量和多样性，以帮助突破具身智能模型固有的数据瓶颈。

在攻读硕士学位前，本人曾于明尼苏达州的卡尔顿学院完成计算机科学和数学双专业本科学习。学术研究之余，本人尤钟情于在户外徒步、讨论哲学问题和创作短篇小说。
